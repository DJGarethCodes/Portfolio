Hobbies

Scuba Diving

I found the love for scuba diving a few years ago when I obtained my diving qualification
as a way to overcome my fear of the ocean and what lies in the never ending abyss.

Since then I have definitely overcome that fear and have enjoyed every moment of it, the
beauty and absolute tranquility I expereince each and every time is my version of inter
planet tourism.

If you have not given this a go yet, contact me and we can dive together.

Cycling - road and off-road

Cycling has always been part of my life, from the social cycling within my local area at a
young age to competing in various races in my adulthood.

Being part of a team has given me the opportunity to increase on social skills and working
within a team enviroment which have assisted me in my career. Teamwork is an indispensable
skill used on a daily basis in every work enviroment whether you are working on-site, hybrid
or remote, you will need to work with people and being able to work well with others in a team
enviroment is definitely an asset.

The physical and mental toll a race take on your body and mind teaches you how to overcome
those limitations you had. Pushing through your limitations and crossing that finish line, beating
your previous years lap and race time is one of the greatest feelings. This then carries over into
your professional career, breaking those boundaries and overcome your limitations.
